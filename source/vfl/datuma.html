Dat.um.a

Dat(um)a is a series of pieces that navigate the individual stories within the scope of a big dataset. Consisting of modules representing different levels of geographic resolution - neighborhoods, census blocks, and street blocks - each piece provides a different way of visualizing the changing cost of apartment rent in Brooklyn.
